My SEO Strategy

I developed and optimized these websites using WordPress, incorporating a comprehensive SEO strategy that led to a top-ranking position in search engine results. The strategy included:

  • **Keyword Research** : Identified high-traffic, low-competition keywords relevant to the homestay industry.
  • **On-Page SEO** : Optimized titles, meta descriptions, headers, and content with target keywords. Ensured all images had descriptive alt tags and proper file names.
  • **Quality Content** : Created engaging and informative content, including blog posts about local attractions and travel tips, to attract and retain visitors.
  • **Mobile Optimization** : Ensured the website was fully responsive and provided a seamless experience on all devices.
  • **Site Speed** : Improved site loading times through image optimization, caching, and leveraging content delivery networks (CDNs).
  • **Local SEO** : Claimed and optimized the Google My Business listing, included local keywords, and encouraged guest reviews.
  • **Technical SEO** : Ensured the website was free of technical issues, had an XML sitemap, and a clean URL structure.
